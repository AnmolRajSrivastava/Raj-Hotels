About Raj Hotels

Welcome to Raj Hotels, where luxury meets traditional Indian hospitality. Since our establishment in 1992, we have been setting the gold standard in premium hospitality across India, with properties nestled in the heart of the country's most prestigious locations. Our architectural masterpieces seamlessly blend contemporary luxury with rich cultural heritage, featuring intricate designs inspired by India's royal past while incorporating modern sophistication and comfort. Our dedicated team of hospitality experts, including world-class chefs, skilled therapists, and personal butlers, works tirelessly to craft unforgettable experiences for our distinguished guests from around the world, ensuring every moment of their stay is marked by impeccable service, extraordinary comfort, and the genuine warmth of Indian hospitality.
